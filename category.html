Categories

Ethnic Wear

Traditional clothing that reflects a particular culture or region. Examples include sarees, salwar kameez, and kurtas.

Casual Wear

Comfortable and informal clothing suitable for everyday use. Think jeans, t-shirts, and sneakers.

Formal Wear

Clothing appropriate for formal events and occasions. Includes suits, dresses, and tuxedos.

Sports Wear

Clothing designed for athletic activities. Examples include tracksuits, leggings, and jerseys.

Western Wear

Clothing styles derived from European fashion. Often includes jeans, button-down shirts, and dresses.
